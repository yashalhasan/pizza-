How will I know when the restaurant received my Order?


Once you submit your order, you will receive a confirmation email at the address provided during registration. And if you are still unsure about your order, you can always call us at 111-241-241.



What payment options are available through Internet Ordering?


We accept cash on delivery and payment through credit card.



I can't find what I'm looking for?


Call us at 111-241-241 and we will help you.



What are the delivery charges?


Our delivery is absolutely free of charge.



Which restaurants offer delivery service?


Most Shale Pizza restaurants offer delivery service. To find out if your hometown Shale Pizza restaurant offers delivery, enter your city into our Store Locator. Any of our restaurants that deliver to the address you entered will be listed.
